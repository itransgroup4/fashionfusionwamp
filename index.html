ABOUT US

Design Fusion is an idea bornout of the need to solve the problem of getting beautiful and finished designs with the idea never ending "disappointing designers","bad tailoring"&"delivery delay". Utilizing this platform we will also grow "the buy Nigeria,grow Nigeria" narrative, while exporting only the finest African materials around the world and partnering to achieve SDG goals(Decent Work and Economic Growth,Infrastructure and Reducing inequlity).These will be achieved by creating a website platform that creates structure for clients,and most importantly,the budding fashion industry of designers,as well as fabric makers and suppliers. Anyone from the ages of 16 years with access to the globe..

Designers,Fabric Makers & Suppliers who sign up on our platform will pay 15% of each item displayed and bougt on our website and social media platforms, and Designerfusion will provide fast and quick access to top-notch designers for any Fashion program, service or product. We have two kind of vendors; The fashion Designers(These people sign-up on our plat form and cover all kinds of designs). The fabric Makers/Suppliers (Distributors):they are the oneswho sign up on our website and showcase their fabrics such as Ankara,Lace,etc. for them to sold in large or small quantities per request within and outside Nigeria. The process of clients experiencing excellent service on our website is;opening our page and then getting choosing any designers(designer rating and history wll be available).

Measurement input tab provide 3 options:(the size chart that details their waist and other size requirements,inputing their own measurement metrics information and getting a Designer to their doorstep at request for a cost). We will also offer follow-up services such as 6-months adjustment periods for clients whose body may change as the designed dresses are also made with allowance..
